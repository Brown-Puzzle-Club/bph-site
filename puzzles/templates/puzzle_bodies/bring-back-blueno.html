INSTRUCTIONS FOR RESURRECTION:
  1. Draw a suitable shape in chalk. (See the diagram below.)
  2. Place your anchoring pieces at the points of the shape.
    1. The MUSICAL anchor should go in the top-left (note: this is a temporary anchor and may not be listed with the others!)
    2. The SEPARATION anchor should go in the top-right
    3. The THOUGHT anchor should go on the right
    4. The STATE anchor should go on the bottom
    5. The SEAT anchor should go on the left
  3. Draw the suitable connections of power between your anchors, along the lines.
  4. Count dramatically, and very, very carefully. Consider only the shape and official title of the anchor. (Visiting the anchors may help.)
  5. Read around the circle, clockwise, to receive your final incantation.
