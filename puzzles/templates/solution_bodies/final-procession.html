That's probably enough to get started! We don't seem to have enough information yet to tackle the first two sections or the appendix, so let's look at the third section. Based on the words used here (SEAT, SEPARATION, BATTLE, etc.), this section is referring to the "anchors" which were used in the very first metapuzzle in this hunt: five different statues located around campus. Now we are equipped to handle the rest of the puzzle. As Put Blueno Back notes, Blueno was placed at the beginning of the procession; indeed, the start of Section 1 details "Placing The Icon Anchor", including specific instructions about hiding it. So, we need to work out where this set of instructions ends, and then work backwards from that endpoint to find Blueno's location. Let's assume we start at where the Society ended up; that is, their temporary headquarters of Sayles 306 (and where the team received their copy of this puzzle!) Starting there, and following the instructions in reverse, we need to...
Instruction Reverse Instruction
GO UP the STAIRS. Go down the stairs, coming out on the Main Green.
PASS THROUGH the ARCH. Head north to pass through the Faunce Archway.
WALK ONE BLOCK. Walk one block north, along Brown Street.
TURN LEFT at BROWN STREET. Turn right, off of Brown Street, onto Angell Street.
TAKE the next possible RIGHT DOWN a STREET. Take a left off of Angell Street, such that you pass a sign about a church before you can make another left turn. The very first left turn works! (IMPORTANT NOTE: during the hunt, the stretch of Olive Street between the northern stretch of The Walk and Brown Street was impassable due to construction.)
WALK SOUTH. Walk north, as you were already doing.
PASS the SIGN about the CHURCH. Pass the sign about the church: which is to say, this one. It's much, much more conspicuous in real life!
Face SOUTHEAST. CROSS the STREET. Cross Meeting Street in a northwest direction.
Stand by the MAP. A big, circular, solid map in the ground... like, say, this one?
We've found the map! Now it's time to overlay the diagram in the appendix over our map. (Make sure that north is up! Which way was is north, again?) The overlay is very, very precise... you may want to pull out a computer, take a good photo, and resize it in photoshop until the thickness of the rim of the Tribute map lines up exactly with the thickness of the line in the diagram. Or you could just plant your finger somewhere and keep trying doors until one of them works. (Solvers generally went with the second option.) As it happens, the red circle lines up directly over... The door into the Morris-Champlin tunnel system. Following the rest of the instructions in order:
Instruction Reverse Instruction
You should come out in the place marked on the MAP (see Appendix 1). Enter through the door marked on the map. (It will open to anyone with Brown University swipe access.)
When you can, TURN RIGHT, and CLIMB the STAIRS. Go down the stairs and turn left.
WALK in the only DIRECTION you can. GO DOWN a gentle slope. Climb up the gentle slope and follow the hallway.
Place the ANCHOR DOWN in your selected location. It should be UP, against a wall. Look above you!
