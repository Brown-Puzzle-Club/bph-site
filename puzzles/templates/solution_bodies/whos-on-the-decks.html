Putting all of this info together, it becomes clear that you will have to use the track numbers to generate your own Spotify Code. You can do this many different ways (photoshop, drawing it out, Google Sheets) and there are numerous Reddit pages that have sections to help guide you. However, a hastily made code probably won't scan successfully. (Most solvers used Photoshop or equivalent open-source software to create the code).
