There are many so-called 'portmanteaus' which don't actually stem from the combination of two words, but are instead created backwards from an existing word. (I like the term "portmanfaux" to refer to one of these.) As a result, I was very careful to confirm that all of the answers used in this meta are actual, real, bona-fide, grade-A genuine portmanteaus. {% endblock %}
